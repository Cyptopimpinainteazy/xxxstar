## Red Flags & Risk Mitigation

### Red Flag 1: "Hardware has undisclosed damage"
- **Mitigation:** Always require pre-delivery inspection photos
- **Recourse:** Reject on delivery, don't pay

### Red Flag 2: "Shipping costs more than expected"
- **Mitigation:** Always get shipping quote before committing
- **Recourse:** Negotiate shipping cost into final price

### Red Flag 3: "Delayed delivery (> 4 weeks)"
- **Mitigation:** Request weekly status updates
- **Recourse:** Have backup sources ready to switch

### Red Flag 4: "Only 50% of units operational"
- **Mitigation:** Request operational rate guarantee (80%+ standard)
- **Recourse:** Negotiate price down based on actual yield
