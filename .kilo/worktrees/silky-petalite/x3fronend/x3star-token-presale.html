FAQ

Common Questions

How do I participate in the presale? +
Connect a supported wallet, enter the amount you want to spend, and follow the checkout flow. Allocation and vesting terms are shown during checkout and in the presale memo.
What is the token vesting schedule? +
Presale tokens follow the published vesting schedule. Round III bonus allocations, if active, follow the same schedule outlined in the presale memo.
When is the TGE (Token Generation Event)? +
TGE timing is announced only after mainnet readiness is confirmed. Participants are notified through official email and community channels.
Is there a minimum purchase? +
Minimums and maximums are shown at checkout and may vary by payment rail and jurisdiction.
Which networks/currencies are accepted? +
Supported networks and payment rails are listed in the live checkout experience and can change by jurisdiction.
