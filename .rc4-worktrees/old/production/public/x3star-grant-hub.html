The winning strategy
How a one-man DARPA lab wins $2.8M without giving up a single point of equity.
Why we qualify
01
Novel architecture = research eligibility
A custom kernel, custom VM, and custom language constitute original research. NSF SBIR and EF grants are specifically designed for exactly this.
02
GPU routing = AI compute research
The GPU swarm architecture overlaps with DOE, DARPA, and together.ai compute research programs. The AI spine is the hook into every AI grant.
03
Post-quantum = government priority
NIST just finalized post-quantum standards. Every government agency is now required to assess quantum-safe infrastructure. We're building it.
04
Multi-VM = Web3 ecosystem value
EVM + SVM + BTC + X3VM atomic execution is a direct application for Ethereum Foundation, Solana Foundation, and Filecoin grants simultaneously.
Application playbook
A
Lead with the benchmark
2.4B intra-node TPS on a home Threadripper. That number opens doors. Lead every application with the hardware spec and the methodology.
B
Frame the AI spine for each audience
For NSF: "novel AI-integrated consensus mechanism." For DARPA: "autonomous adaptive compute architecture." For EF: "AI-guided execution VM." Same tech, right words.
C
Publish the methodology first
Open-source the benchmark methodology before applying. Grant committees trust reproducible research. It also creates community validators.
D
Apply in parallel, not sequence
All 18 programs simultaneously. They don't conflict. The same IP can receive multiple grants from different agencies for different aspects of the same work.
