HOW YOU
EARN.

1
PRIMARY
ComitV2 Weight Fees

Every cross-chain call through X3-kernel's submitComitV2 incurs a weight fee. Validators processing these extrinsics earn proportional fees from the X3 planned fee market — stable and predictable.

2
SECONDARY
HTLC Relay Bonuses

Validator-run relayer nodes that detect secret reveals and broadcast cross-chain HTLC claims earn relay bonuses. Speed is rewarded: first valid claim relay collects the bonus.

3
PASSIVE
Flashloan Pool Share

Validators may opt into the multi-provider flashloan registry. Balancer V2 (0% fee, high liquidity) and Kamino (Solana, 0%) allocate pool rewards to liquidity contributors.

4
MEV ADVANTAGE
Priority Ordering Rights

X3's Flash Finality and encrypted mempool give validators first-mover advantage on cross-chain arb windows. With 200ms finality vs ETH's 60s, X3 validators close arb before competitors see it.

5
STAKING
Collateral Vault Yield

X3-lang vault contracts allow validators to deposit collateral that backs atomic swaps. In exchange, vaults generate yield from swap execution fees and cross-chain PnL settlement.

6
GOVERNANCE
Protocol Governance

Validator nodes participate in protocol upgrades, fee parameter voting, and flash provider registry decisions. Early validators carry weighted governance as the ecosystem scales.

Security

ATTACK
VECTORS.

Frontrunning
Secret S stays off-chain until both flashlegs succeed. Flashbots/private relay and high gas tips prevent extraction. X3 encrypted mempool adds additional layer.
MITIGATED
Chain Reorg
12 EVM block confirms, 5 SVM confirms, X3 flash finality (<400ms). Relayer detects orphaned TX and either rebroadcasts or triggers timeout refund.
MITIGATED
Partial Fill
Atomic executor guarantees all legs succeed or zero execute. If one flashleg reverts, no secret is revealed. Both HTLCs refund after respective timelocks expire.
MITIGATED
Oracle Manip.
Uses AMM pool prices and TWAPs instead of centralized feeds. Trade size limited to <5% of pool depth to prevent single-trade price manipulation.
MITIGATED
Censorship
Multi-party forwarding + X3 Private Mempool feature. ComitV2 submissions can route through multiple RPC nodes if one validator censors.
PARTIAL
Inventory Drain
Max loan size capped at 50% of vault inventory. Reserves diversified across assets. Automated alerts on imbalance with multi-sig kill-switch available.
MITIGATED
Fee Spike
Relayer continuously updates gas estimates and resubmits. If deadline approaches, it escalates priority. If expired, treated as failure — full refund triggered.
