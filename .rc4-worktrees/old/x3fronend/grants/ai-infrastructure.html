StarSignal

AI infrastructure for safer multi-VM settlement.

StarSignal is the AI layer for X3 Atomic Star. It helps route across BTC, EVM, SVM, and X3VM, score liquidity risk, detect validator anomalies, and produce security-aware execution receipts.

Fund AI infrastructureRequest technical demo

Problem

Multi-chain execution is noisy, fragmented, and risky. Funders need evidence that routing decisions are based on real-time risk, validator health, and execution quality — not just optimistic pathing.

X3 solution

StarSignal analyzes liquidity, routing paths, validator behavior, congestion, and execution outcomes across domains. It helps X3 make safer decisions and generates machine-readable risk receipts for funders.

Route optimizer

Chooses execution paths that minimize risk, gas impact, and supply drift across domains.

Risk classifier

Scores liquidity pools, bridge paths, and validator choices against modelled threat conditions.

Technical modules
