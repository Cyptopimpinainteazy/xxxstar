BTC Fortress

Bringing BTC liquidity into atomic multi-VM settlement.

BTC Fortress is X3 Atomic Star’s Bitcoin settlement track. It creates a controlled xBTC route into the Atomic Kernel, STARDEX, and multi-VM execution domains with proof-backed settlement receipts.

Problem

Bitcoin liquidity is enormous but underused in programmable multi-VM DeFi. Funders want an infrastructure path that respects Bitcoin reserves while making them available to EVM/SVM/X3VM settlement.

X3 solution

X3 uses a BTC lock/watch layer and xBTC representation to bring Bitcoin-backed liquidity into the Atomic Kernel. That liquidity can then flow through STARDEX and execution adapters while preserving canonical supply accounting.

Lock/watch layer

Monitor BTC testnet activity and mint xBTC honors without expanding supply.

STARDEX route

Feed Bitcoin liquidity into native DeFi with controlled route receipts and settlement auditing.
