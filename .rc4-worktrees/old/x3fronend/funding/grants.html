How to use this portal

The portal is designed for reviewers who want a quick path to the funding lane they care about. Each page contains the ask, the technical scope, deliverables, and how the work fits into the X3 Atomic Star infrastructure roadmap.

Next step

Contact the funding team with the lane name and ask for a packet. Example subject: "X3 Atomic Star Funding packet — Post-Quantum Security lane".

This portal is purpose-built to keep funding discussions technical, transparent, and ready for a future subdomain launch.
