SBIR / Defense Funding

Fund resilient, audited infrastructure for critical operations.

This lane targets government and defense-aligned funding that values verified distributed systems, operational readiness, and secure execution environments.

Scope

The SBIR / Defense lane focuses on infrastructure hardening, system integrity, distributed governance, and secure validator and bridge operations.

  • Resilient validator deployment and secure telemetry for critical systems.
  • Proof-driven integrity reports for settlement, cross-domain attestations, and chain security.
  • Defense readiness artifacts for operational assurance and audit review.
  • Governance and control model validation for multi-stakeholder deployments.

Deliverables

Technical deliverables

Infrastructure security reports, resilience model, audit readiness packet, and operational playbooks for secure deployments.

Funding ask

Engineering support, secure infrastructure credits, and resources for defense-grade validation and documentation.

Impact

Supports credible infrastructure funding for mission critical systems with strong operational and security evidence.

How to engage

This lane is intended for grant sponsors who require evidence-first technical planning and operational integrity before commitment.
