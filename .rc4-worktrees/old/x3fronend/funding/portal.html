Email reputation

Operational workflow

  • Public reviewers land on the funding hub and choose a lane.
  • The funding system presents lane-specific requirements, deliverables, and contact paths.
  • Grant teams and providers access a partner portal for intake and evidence submission.
  • Admins monitor progress, approvals, and funding milestones through the scoreboard.

Built-in features

Subdomain readiness

Start under `.org`, then migrate to `funding.x3atomicstar.org` or `funding.x3atomicstar.com` without rewriting content.

Identity & access

Separate public portal from protected admin/provider flows using VPN/SSO or private network access.

Scoreboard tracking

Scoreboard pages frame eligibility, funding status, and evidence score for transparency.

Audit proof

Funding lanes are built on technical deliverables, not marketing claims, to support real review and sponsor diligence.

How to use it now

Run this portal from the existing `.org` site while the system matures. Later the same pages can be served from a dedicated funding subdomain with provider/admin routing layered on top.
