Colorado Funding

Fund local infrastructure, jobs, and economic impact in Colorado.

This lane is designed for Colorado-based grants, workforce development dollars, and regional infrastructure sponsorships.

Scope

Colorado funding supports local deployment, workforce training, hardware recycling programs, and state-level partnerships for distributed infrastructure.

  • Local validator lab and testnet infrastructure in Colorado.
  • Workforce training and technical recruiting for state-based jobs.
  • Partnerships with Colorado research institutions and innovation programs.
  • Regional infrastructure grants that support economic development and resilient systems.

Deliverables

Technical deliverables

Local deployment plan, workforce impact model, infrastructure readiness roadmap, and regional collaboration packet.

Funding ask

Support for local lab space, community hiring, training, and Colorado-friendly state infrastructure programs.

Impact

Strengthens Colorado’s role in secure infrastructure and positions X3 Atomic Star as a local innovation partner.

How to engage

State and regional funding stakeholders can use this lane to back a grounded infrastructure program with local economic benefits.
